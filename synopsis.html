Synopsis

Amara never expected to wake up in someone else’s life. But after transferring bodies with a student across the world, she’s thrown into an unfamiliar education system—one that challenges everything she thought she knew about opportunity, privilege, and the future. As she jumps from student to student, witnessing the past and glimpsing a future she can’t control, she realizes one truth: the fight for education is bigger than her, and she may be the only one who can change it.
